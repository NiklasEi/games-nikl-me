Welcome to my HTML-5 games

The games can be played here in the browser, or directly in Telegram through my bot.

This website including all games is open source. You can help me improving it on GitHub.

Minesweeper Find mines with mathematics. A number on a tile gives you the amount of mine on the surrounding fields.
Sudoku Place numbers in every slot. Each block has to have all number from 1-9. The same has to be true for every row and column.
